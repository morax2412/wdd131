About Daniela

A calm, professional space to work through anxiety, stress, and relationship challenges with practical tools and clear progress goals.

Lic. Daniela Estefany Rondan Suazo • Clinical Psychology

Professional Focus

Lic. Daniela Estefany Rondan Suazo offers psychological support focused on emotional regulation, stress management, anxiety-related concerns, and healthier relationship dynamics. Sessions are structured, respectful, and centered on your goals.

The work is oriented toward helping clients understand patterns, develop coping skills, improve communication, and build long-term emotional resilience using evidence-informed strategies.

Specialties

  • Anxiety and stress management
  • Emotional regulation and coping strategies
  • Self-esteem and personal development
  • Relationship and communication support

Therapy Approach

The approach is collaborative and practical. Sessions may include guided reflection, skill-building, and simple exercises to apply between sessions. Progress is reviewed regularly to ensure the work stays aligned with your goals.

Clarity

We identify the root issues and patterns that keep problems repeating, so you can act with more awareness and control.

Tools

You learn techniques for managing emotions, reducing stress, and improving daily functioning in realistic ways.

Progress

Sessions are focused on measurable improvements, with check-ins and adjustments over time.

Values

  • Confidentiality: Respectful and private sessions.
  • Empathy: A non-judgmental space to speak freely.
  • Professionalism: Clear structure, punctuality, and ethical practice.
  • Evidence-informed support: Practical methods that are widely used in therapy.
